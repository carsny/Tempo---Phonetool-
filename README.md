Tempo → Phonetool Self-Recognition Tool
A Tampermonkey userscript that captures your weekly Shift Utilization and Red Zone data from Tempo and logs it as a performance widget on Phonetool — with streak tracking and rank medals.

This is a self-recognition tool only. Your data is visible to you alone and does not affect any official records or reporting.

How it works
Captures SU and RZ data from Tempo's Shift Overview Lite
Displays a live performance widget on your Phonetool profile
Tracks your weekly streak and ranks you Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum
Lets you document low RZ weeks so they still count toward your streak
